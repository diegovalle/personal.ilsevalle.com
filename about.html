About Me

Who am I? I'll try to briefly describe myself. I was a student at the Escuela de Dietetica y Nutricion in Mexico City. I studied nutrition, which means I'm very interested in how nutrients make our body work. In addition I love teaching and currently work giving Business English classes. I enjoy helping people learn by making my classes interesting and meaningful; the mind is a wonderful tool.

I read all kind of books, but the genres I enjoy the most are science fiction (the Harry Potter kind, not the Star-Trek) and novels. I also enjoy reading science articles from Medical and Nutrition Journals. I enjoy a wide variety of music from Lenny Kravitz, Aerosmith, and Shakira to The Beatles. I like fine wine, good food (my brother besides being incredibly handsome is a great cook) and sports. The kind of wine I like the most is made from the grape "Merlot" (I took a Diploma Course in Enology, so I enjoy sampling different wines). For my work outs, I mostly run or do ergometer every day—I'm addicted to endorphins.

My favorite book of all-time is El libro secreto de Grazia dei Rossi by Jaqueline Park. Other favorites include: Summer Sisters, by Judy Blume; Where the Heart Is, by Billie Letts; Corazón, by Edmundo de Amicis; and Galileo's Daughter, by Dava Sobel.

I like going out, mainly to the movies, but I also like to visit museums, theaters, or to just hang out with my friends. My favorite movies are "Dead Poet's Society" and "The Last Unicorn". I'm also a Star Wars fan.

Three things which I like and always put me in a good mood are wine, chocolate and sunflowers.
